Our Experiences


First and foremost, our experiences during our work immersion were nothing short of interesting. We gained valuable knowledge about what to expect after completing our studies. Experiencing this early on allowed us to realize things we hadn't fully understood until now. Though, truthfully, much of our time during the immersion was spent waiting, the experiences we gained during those moments were nonetheless priceless.

First Day

Today marks the first day of our immersion program, and we had the privilege of attending the flag ceremony alongside the city officials and employees. It was a meaningful experience as we witnessed the formal proceedings and the sense of unity and patriotism among the participants. This opportunity allowed us to gain insight into the local government's structure and operations while reinforcing the importance of discipline, respect, and civic duty. We look forward to the learnings and experiences that this immersion will bring in the days ahead.

Office

We are currently waiting for our tour guide, Ma'am Jackie, who is one of the dedicated employees of the Information and Communications Technology Office (ICTO). She will be guiding us through our tour, providing valuable insights into the department's functions, operations, and contributions to the city's technological advancements. We are excited to learn from her expertise and gain a deeper understanding of how ICTO plays a crucial role in the city's digital infrastructure and services.
